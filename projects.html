E-Commerce Platform with Admin Dashboard

Final Year Project

Project Overview

A full-featured e-commerce platform designed to provide seamless shopping experiences with robust admin controls for inventory and order management.

Technologies Used

React.js Node.js Express.js MongoDB Redux JWT Authentication Stripe Payment

Problem Statement

Small and medium-sized businesses struggle with managing online sales due to complex and expensive e-commerce solutions. They need an affordable, user-friendly platform that integrates inventory management, payment processing, and customer analytics in one place.

Unique Proposition

  • Real-time inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
  • Integrated analytics dashboard for sales insights
  • Multi-vendor support with individual seller dashboards
  • Automated email notifications for orders and shipments
  • Mobile-responsive design for seamless shopping on any device

Competitor Analysis

Shopify

Strength: Extensive app marketplace and templates

Weakness: High monthly fees and transaction costs

WooCommerce

Strength: Free and open-source with WordPress integration

Weakness: Requires technical knowledge for setup and maintenance

BigCommerce

Strength: Built-in SEO and multi-channel selling

Weakness: Limited design flexibility and complex pricing tiers

Our Edge: We combine affordability with powerful features, offering real-time analytics and multi-vendor capabilities without the complexity or high costs of competitors.

Restaurant Management System

Project Overview

A modern restaurant website featuring online ordering, table reservations, and menu management capabilities designed to enhance customer experience and streamline operations.

Technologies Used

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Bootstrap 5 jQuery

Problem Statement

Restaurants face challenges in managing customer orders, table reservations, and menu updates efficiently. Traditional phone-based ordering leads to errors and inefficiency, while customers expect convenient online ordering options.

Unique Proposition

  • Interactive menu with filtering by category and dietary preferences
  • Real-time table availability checker
  • Online ordering with customization options
  • Reservation system with email confirmations
  • Gallery showcase of dishes and restaurant ambiance

Competitor Analysis

OpenTable

Strength: Large user base and trusted brand

Weakness: High commission fees per reservation

Toast

Strength: Comprehensive POS integration

Weakness: Expensive hardware requirements

Our Edge: Lightweight, affordable solution focused on customer-facing features without expensive hardware or commission fees.
